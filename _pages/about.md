Welcome to my homepage! I am a second-year master's student at the University of Southern California, majoring in computer science. My research interests lie in **edge AI**, focusing on both efficient and adaptive on-device AI models, and learning-based optimization methods for multi-agent edge systems. Ultimately, I seek to enable edge AI systems that are efficient, adaptive, and reliable in dynamic real-world settings.
